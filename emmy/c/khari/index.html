Khari di Baandari

Dominion Major

Basic Info

An Elsweyr native, born to a family of rich Baandari merchants, Khari has never been left wanting for material possessions. Bold, unwavering, and seeking fun and glory in all things, he ventured from the family manor in pursuit of a glory-filled life on the front lines.

After many years of training, countless battles fought, and sticking like glue to a first-unwitting “mate,” Khari has finally come to settle amidst the plains and sands of Reaper’s March… Sort of.
